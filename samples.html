📝 Sample MCQs

 Boeing 737NG
Q1: Which failure during an ILS approach causes the autopilot to disengage immediately?
  • A) Loss of both radio altimeters below 200 feet AGL
  • B) Loss of IRS data during localizer alignment
  • C) Course deviation exceeding 5 degrees
Answer: A
Source: DFCS Manual | ATA-22 | Page 150
Q2: What does the FCC do if it identifies an erroneous radio altimeter signal during an ILS approach?
  • A) Disconnects autopilot
  • B) Uses a secondary radio altimeter
  • C) Ignores the altitude input
Answer: B
Source: DFCS Manual | ATA-22 | Page 150
 Airbus A320
Q1: What does the Terrain Clearance Floor (TCF) mode in EGPWS do?
  • A) Decreases terrain clearance
  • B) Increases terrain clearance above airport runway
  • C) Changes alert generation altitude
Answer: B
Source: EGPWS Manual | Page 106
